Albany Middle School

421 Trident Drive

Havana, Cuba



April 21 2003

10 years ago, my boss gave me lots of money for my hardwork and I decided to go on a cruise from Point Pierre to Cape Verde. But, things went horribly wrong and I became a victim of a disaster. Luckily on the journey I made a map of where we’ve been because I always wanted to know where I’ve been. Art was always a hidden talent of mine so, when I was paranoid, I created an art piece to capture the beauty of the storm.

In my time being in the triangle, I found out the sea is not a kind place and it will strip you of your life. Being on the cruise ship Dues Avon, I wrote a diary of what I experienced here but of course, my thoughts appeared to be wrong. For example with my own eyes, I saw swirling clouds hit the front of the ship which made it off balanced. From that experience, I learned about changes in the air which can impact the buoyancy of a vessel. Ship safety and procedures are also a big part of my involvement with the tragedy so, I learned about what ships do to become safe from storms and massive waves. After surviving the catastrophe, I wanted to know about other stories for full knowledge of the Bermuda Triangle. One particular survivor had theories similar to mine so, I added a short story to fully explain the theories. Since I had so much knowledge about the Bermuda Triangle, I created this website to tell everyone about my near-death experience. But, not everyone knows all the complex words so, I added a glossary on the navigation bar.

The experience of being in the Bermuda Triangle still haunts me today but, I can be thankful that I survived. Before the accident, I was being paid very little. But now, I’m an expert on the Triangle so, lots of channels come to me for advice and facts. Making a website was challenging but it was worth it. One of the things I’m very proud of is the tedious amount of research and papers I had to make. But, finding people who would take my research was hard because, many news channels thought I was lying. Concluding this multi-genre portfolio, I want to know more about how airplanes and ships handle emergencies like these.

Sincerely,











Xavier Ford
