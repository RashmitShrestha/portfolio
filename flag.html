The Flag of Aequalis

Injustice clouds our vision, let's rise up and shine!



This is a flag representing the equality, solidarity, and diversity that we should try and proliferate in the world.


What am I looking at?

Every aspect of the flag (color, symbols, etc) has a deepeer meaning following the central themes mentioned above.


Color

The 2 colors, red and blue, represent opposites found throught the world: hot and cold, start and end of the color spectrum, even republican and democrat. This shows how we can all coexist in peace; we might be different but also, we share this earth together so we must learn to help each other While the two different shades of gray represent the mix of white and black, which are also opposites but people forget that they can also be combined, representing the unification of races. Going even deeper, the blue and red are pastel colors because they represent happiness and are generally soothing. The colors were chosen to amalgamate, while representing different sections of the color wheel.


Symbols

The overall depiction of the flag is subjective, but this is my thought process: The gray rectangle and triangle represent a scale, while the figures on the opposite sides of the scale are allusions of people. Historically, the scale also represents justice (for victims of racial bigotry for example) and fairness.There's also an apparent equals sign on both of them, showing how they're both equal. In addition, the colors mismatched onto them dispute ideas of segregation and instead promote solidarity and unity. You might've also noticed that the flag isn't a rectangle, it looks like a glorified puzzle piece, doesn't it? This unconventional shape represents diversity among the amalgamation of existing flags, but it also shows that we are a part of something greater, we're only a piece of the puzzle but even if one piece goes missing, then the puzzle won't be complete.


What's Next?

Just like all of the other flags being used to spread equality, unity, and solidarity, I hope this flag will fly high with all of the other flags, until the sky is illuminated with a symphony of pristine colors. I hope we all get to bathe in those colors, united and ready to fight all injustices.
