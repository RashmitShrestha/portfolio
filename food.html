Delicious Momo!

What is a momo?

A momo is a steamed dumpling filled with meat of vegetables.

If you want to make momo, here is what you will need...

  • 2 lbs of ground chicken or pork
  • 1 cup chopped red onion
  • 1 cup chopped green onion
  • 1 cup chopped cabbage
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 3 tsp. momo masala
  • 1/2 tsp. red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp. paprika powder
  • 2 tbsp. melted butter
  • 1 tsp. garlic ginger paste
  • 1 tsp. fenugreek seed
  • 1 tsp. turmeric powder
  • 5 tbsp. oil
  • Momo wrapper


When you get all your ingredients this is what you'll need to/

  1. Mix up all the ingredients except oil and fenugreek seeds in a bowl
  2. Take a small frying pan and heat the oil and fenugreek seeds until turned black
  3. Then mix the black fenugreek and oil into the mixture.
  4. Then you need to wrap the kima in the momo wrapper.
  5. After making the raw momos, get a momo steamer and boil on high for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
